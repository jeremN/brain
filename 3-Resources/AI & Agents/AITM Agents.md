# AITM Agents

**Source:** [https://www.aitmpl.com/agents](https://www.aitmpl.com/agents)
**Category:** AI & Agents

## Summary
A framework for Agents In The Middle (AITM) - autonomous AI agents that sit between systems to orchestrate, mediate, and enhance workflows. Enables sophisticated multi-agent coordination and decision-making.
## Why It's Interesting
A critical architectural pattern for enterprise AI automation and system integration.
## Key Takeaways
- Positions agents as intermediaries between systems
- Enables intelligent workflow orchestration
- Supports complex multi-agent scenarios
- Reduces manual intervention in automated processes
