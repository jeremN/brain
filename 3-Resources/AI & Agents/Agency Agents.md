## Summary
A framework or toolkit for building AI agents with autonomous decision-making capabilities and goal-oriented behavior. Focuses on giving agents the ability to take actions aligned with defined objectives.
## Why It's Interesting
Enables creating truly autonomous AI systems that can operate with minimal human supervision.

- Action validation and safety mechanisms
- Integration with external tools and APIs
