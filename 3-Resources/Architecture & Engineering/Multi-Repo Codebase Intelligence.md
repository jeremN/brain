# Multi-Repo Codebase Intelligence

**Source:** [https://josephmosby.com/notes-on-a-multi-repo-codebase-intelligence-system/](https://josephmosby.com/notes-on-a-multi-repo-codebase-intelligence-system/)
**Category:** Architecture & Engineering

## Summary
Explores tools and techniques for maintaining code intelligence and tooling across multiple repositories. Addresses challenges of monorepos, polyrepos, and hybrid approaches.
## Why It's Interesting
Becomes increasingly important as organizations scale and adopt distributed development practices.
## Key Takeaways
- Multi-repo setups require sophisticated tooling
- Cross-repo analysis enables better refactoring
- Dependency tracking across repos is critical
- AI can help analyze and understand polyrepo relationships
