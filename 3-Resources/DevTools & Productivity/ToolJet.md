# ToolJet

**Source:** [https://github.com/tooljet/tooljet](https://github.com/tooljet/tooljet)
**Category:** DevTools & Productivity

## Summary

ToolJet is an open-source AI-powered platform for building full-stack enterprise internal apps, AI agents, and workflows in minutes. It provides a visual app builder with drag-and-drop components, connects to databases and APIs, and now includes AI-powered generation from natural language prompts. Designed for building internal tools 10x faster than traditional development.

## Why It's Interesting

Leading open-source low-code platform now with AI generation — strong alternative to Retool for internal tools

## Key Takeaways

- All-in-one platform: visual app builder + automation agents + database + workflow engine
- AI-powered: describe what you need in natural language and generate apps from prompts
- Open source with enterprise features — connects to existing databases and APIs
- Designed specifically for internal tools: admin panels, dashboards, CRUD apps, approval workflows
