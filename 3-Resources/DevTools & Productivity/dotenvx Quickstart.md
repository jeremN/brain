# dotenvx Quickstart

**Source:** [https://dotenvx.com/docs/quickstart](https://dotenvx.com/docs/quickstart)
**Category:** DevTools & Productivity

## Summary
A next-generation dotenv tool with encryption and secret management capabilities. Enables secure handling of environment variables with versioning and audit trails.
## Why It's Interesting
Modernizes secret management beyond simple .env files.
## Key Takeaways
- Encryption for sensitive environment variables
- Versioning and audit capabilities
- Team-friendly secret sharing
- Successor to traditional dotenv
